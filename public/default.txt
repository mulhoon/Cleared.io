Welcome to Cleared:
The notepad with superpowers.

Editing Cleared is the same as editing a text file, except Cleared recognises things like headings, todo lists, tags and links.

☐ create a todo item by typing a dash+space on a new line 
☐ check it off by tapping the checkbox #done
☐ use tags to organise your notes #design
